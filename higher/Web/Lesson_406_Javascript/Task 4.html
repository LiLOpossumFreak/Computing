Hornet

Hornet is the daughter of The Pale King and Hera,
thus making her half wyrm and half weaver. In Silksong she is
captured upon Grand Mother Silk's request and taken to Pharloom due to her ability to produce silk.

Lace

Lace is the daughter of Grand Mother Silk.
She is entirely made of silk unlike most other bugs in
the Silksong/Hollow Knight universe. She was created by
Grand Mother Silk to be loyal to GMS and wake her from the
deep sleep the weavers put her in. However she grows to
resent her mother and later and, if you trap GMS in the
Snail Shamans' snare, she goes on to betray her in order to
save Hornet from being dragged into the void with GMS
